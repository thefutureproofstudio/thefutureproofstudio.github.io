Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need any technical background?
None at all. If you can use email and a browser, you're ready. The workshop is designed for people whose expertise is in education, not technology.
Is this relevant to my discipline?
Yes — the workshop draws on examples across engineering, humanities, professional fields, and online programs. It's explicitly not built for one type of institution or discipline.
I'm an instructional designer, not faculty. Is this for me?
Absolutely. The Designer Track is built specifically for IDs and learning designers, with content on rapid course development, faculty support, and AI in the design process.
How long does it take to complete?
About 2.5 hours of video content total. Most participants spread it across a week, watching one or two modules at a time. There's no deadline — work at your own pace.
Will this become outdated quickly?
The frameworks — how to evaluate tools, design policy, support adoption — are durable. Full Bundle purchasers get lifetime updates as the content evolves with the landscape.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes. Complete the workshop and if you don't find it useful, we'll refund you within 30 days — no questions asked.
